JuiceCrew Rent Reminder Opt-In

By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive automated SMS reminders about rent due dates, late rent notices, and important updates related to your lease or rental property.

How to Opt In:

  • Text JOIN to [Your Toll-Free Number] to subscribe.
  • Provide your phone number when signing your lease agreement.
  • Register through the tenant portal and check the SMS consent box.

Message Frequency: Up to 4 reminders per month.

Message & Data Rates: Standard message and data rates may apply.

Opt Out: Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe.

Help: Reply HELP for assistance.
